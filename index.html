What I do

With expertise in data-driven design, I create innovative experiences by integrating data, research, and prototyping. I transform visions into intuitive interfaces aligned with strategic objectives. My focus is not only on designing products but also on telling impactful stories through user-oriented and inclusive design.

Strategic Design Leadership

Guiding, aligning, and inspiring teams in crafting innovative solutions, bridging strategy and design to achieve business objectives effectively.

Design de Experiência do Usuário (UX)

Shaping seamless and delightful user interactions through thoughtful design, optimizing experiences to meet both user needs and business goals.

Design de Interface do Usuário (UI)

Crafting visually appealing and intuitive user interfaces, blending aesthetics and functionality for a compelling and engaging digital experience.

Continuous Evaluation and Iteration

Ongoing assessment and refinement to ensure constant improvement and adaptation in response to changing needs and circumstances.

Team Management and Collaboration

Effective leadership and collaborative coordination within a team to optimize performance, foster innovation, and achieve collective goals.
